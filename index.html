Hi! I'm Amey.

A fullstack developer with major focus on backend. I currently work as a Senior Fullstack Developer at Ernst and Young. For the last 3 years, I have been delivering end to end cloud-ready enterprise applications involving frontend, backend and CI/CD components.

I'm well versed in CQRS/Event sourcing/Domain Driven Design paradigm. I have worked in Java, Spring, Hibernate, Spring-cloud and Axon framework to deliver these event driven/event sourced applications. I am well aware of microservice architecture and deploying the applications to cloud using Openshift. I also have experience in interfacing these application with React and AngularJS frontends.

In my leisure time, I also work on my personal projects. I encourage you to have a look at my GitHub profile or portfolio section for more on this. One such fascinating project I am working on is MEERA, an open source, general purpose highly configurable AI powered virtual assistant.

When I am not coding, I spend my time listening to rock and folk-metal music and reading books from my library on Kindle. Check out my Goodreads profile for more.
