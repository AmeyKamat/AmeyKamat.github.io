Winner at Semicolons 2015

Semicolon 2015 was a 24-hours global hackathon by Persistent Systems. We as "Team Optimus Goa" developed a platform for social cause collaboration using nodejs and Bootstrap. It included a project-centric model where people could join an event and contribute to the social cause.
