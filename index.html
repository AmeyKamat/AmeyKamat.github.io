Who is Amey?

Amey Kamat is a Ponda, Goa based Software Engineer and Developer. He did his B. Tech (Computer Science and Engineering) from National Institute of Technology, Goa in 2016. Amey has worked previously in Persistent Systems Limited and IIT Bombay on projects in wide spectrum of fields including AI, Software Development, Backend Development and Web Designing. Recently his paper based on CNC automation has been accepted in International Conference on Industrial Technology 2016 (ICIT 2016), Taipei, Taiwan. He has also been developer of choice for web development for NIT Goa including Technival 2016 and First Convocaion website of NIT Goa.

Apart from development, Amey has worked as secretary for Laser Mission 10x, an initiative by NIT Goa to develop scientific attitude in school children, for which he was featured in Navhind Times. He loves rock music, have avid interest in history and enjoy reading historical thrillers.

Technical Skill Set

With 4 years of experience, Amey's skill set is broad and adaptable for any kind of software project work. He has worked mainly in C/C++, Python and Java with varying levels of complexity. He is also familiar with HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, Bootstrap, JQuery and AngularJS.
